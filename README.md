# Aflami Android App

Aflami is an Android app for movie and TV show lovers. It helps you discover, track, and explore a huge library of entertainment. With a clean interface, Aflami makes it easy to find the perfect movie or show to watch based on your mood and viewing history. This project is your one-stop-shop for everything movies and TV.

## Features

### Authentication
*   **Splash Screen:** An engaging entry point with an animated icon and text.
*   **Login:** Secure user login.
*   **Create Account:** Easy new user registration.
*   **Forgot Password:** A simple process to recover a forgotten password.

### Home Screen
The home screen is your personalized dashboard for discovering new and interesting content.
*   **Popular Section:** A slider showcasing popular movies and TV shows with their genres displayed prominently under each title.
*   **Recently Watched:** This section keeps track of the movies and TV shows you've viewed the details of. An "All" button allows you to see an expanded list of your recently viewed content.
*   **Top Rating Section:** Discover critically acclaimed movies and TV shows. An "All" button provides access to a more extensive list of top-rated content.
*   **Mood Picker:** Unsure what to watch? Select your current mood, and Aflami will suggest a movie for you. You can either dive into its details or get another suggestion until you find the perfect match.
*   **Upcoming Section:** Stay ahead of the curve by browsing upcoming movies and TV shows, conveniently categorized by genre.

### Advanced Search
The powerful search functionality allows you to find exactly what you're looking for.
*   **Search by Keyword:** Find movies and TV shows by any keyword. Results display the title, poster image, release year, and rating.
*   **Filter Options:** Refine your keyword search results by rating or genre.
*   **Search by Actor:** Enter an actor's name to see a list of their movies.
*   **Search by Country:** Discover movies from a specific country. Start typing a country's name to see suggestions, and select one to view related films.
*   **Recent Searches:** Your recent search keywords are saved for quick access.

### Details Screens
Get all the information you need on a dedicated screen for each movie and TV show.

**Movie Details:**
*   **Image Carousel:** An auto-playing slider of high-quality images at the top of the screen.
*   **Core Information:** View the movie's title, rating, genre, description, release date, and runtime.
*   **Cast:** See the main cast and navigate to a full cast screen.
*   **More Like This:** A section suggesting movies similar to the one you are viewing.
*   **Reviews:** Read reviews from other users.
*   **Gallery:** Browse all images related to the movie.
*   **Production Companies:** See the posters of the companies involved in the movie's production.
*   **Video Player:** Watch trailers and other related videos.
*   **User Rating:** Rate the movie on a scale of 1 to 5.
*   **Personalized Lists:** Add the movie to custom lists you create.

**TV Show Details:**
*   **Image Carousel:** An auto-playing slider of stunning images.
*   **Core Information:** Access the TV show's title, rating, genre, description, and release date.
*   **Cast:** View the primary cast and navigate to see the full cast list.
*   **More Like This:** Discover TV shows similar to the one you are currently viewing.
*   **Reviews:** Read user-submitted reviews.
*   **Gallery:** A collection of all images associated with the TV show.
*   **Production Companies:** View the posters of the production companies behind the show.
*   **Video Player:** Play trailers and other related videos.
*   **User Rating:** Give the TV show a rating out of 5.
*   **Seasons and Episodes:**
    *   Browse through all the seasons of the TV show.
    *   View a list of all episodes for a selected season, including the episode title, duration, image, and rating.
    *   Play the video for each episode directly.
    *   A dedicated section for any special episodes is also available.
